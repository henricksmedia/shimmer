Quick Start


    

        What is shimmer? A thin, fizzy, metallic noise that AI
        music tools add to the high end. Shimmer finds that noise and
        turns it down, leaving your music alone.
    


    
How to use it

    
    
        
  1. 1 Drop or pick an audio file.
    2. 
        
  2. 2 Pick a preset, or click Auto Detect.
    3. 
        
  3. 3 Click Process.
    4. 
        
  4. 4 Compare Original and Processed.
    5. 
        
  5. 5 Play Removed to hear what was taken out.
    6. 
    


    

        Play the Removed track. It should sound like fizz or
        hiss. If you hear music in it, you cut too hard — raise

Which preset should I use?

                

                    Answer a couple of questions and we'll pick one for you.
                    Or click Auto Detect on the main panel to let
                    Shimmer listen to your file and decide.
                

                

            


            

                
What each control does

                

                    The preset already sets these for you. Tweak only if
                    something sounds off. Each card tells you when to turn
                    a knob up or down.
                

                

            


            

                
Common problems

                

                    Quick fixes for the things that go wrong most often.
                


                

                    
The result sounds dull or lifeless

                    
You cut too hard. Raise Threshold, lower
                    Slope, or drop Mix to about 70–80%.

                


                

                    
Bass or kick feels thin

                    
Turn Denoise and De-resonator down.
                    Shimmer never lives in the bass, so these don't need
                    to be high.

                


                

                    
Still too much fizz

                    
Raise De-harsh, lower Threshold a
                    little, or switch to a stronger preset like
                    suno_v5 or suno_v5.5.

                


                

                    
Weird warble or pumping sound

                    
Lower Denoise. When it's too strong it adds
                    its own artifacts.

                


                

                    
The Removed track has music in it

                    
Shimmer is grabbing real music. Raise Threshold
                    or lower Slope until Removed sounds
                    like just hiss or fizz.

                

            


            

                
Setup for MP3 and M4A files

                

                    WAV, FLAC, and OGG files work out of the box. MP3 and
                    M4A need a free helper called ffmpeg.
                


                

                    
Install ffmpeg on Windows

                    
Open a terminal and run:

                    
winget install ffmpeg

                    
Then restart Shimmer.

                


                

                    
Other systems

                    

                        Download from
                        ffmpeg.org
                        and add it to your PATH.
